HistorYan
July 5, 1945

The Liberation Communique

• Gen. Douglas MacArthur declares the full liberation of the Philippines.
• Official announcement marks the conclusion of major WWII combat operations.
• Honors the combined victory of allied forces and local guerrilla movements.

July 7, 1892

The Founding of the Katipunan

• Andres Bonifacio establishes the underground secret society (KKK) in San Nicolas, Manila.
• Formed on the exact day Jose Rizal's exile to Dapitan was made public.
• Marks the pivotal shift from peaceful reform to armed revolution.

July 7, 1864

Birth of Isabelo de los Reyes

• Born "Don Belong" in Vigan, Ilocos Sur to prominent poetess Leona Florentino.
• Celebrated historically as the "Father of Filipino Socialism."
• Co-founded the Philippine Independent Church (Aglipayan Church).

July 10, 1934

The 1934 Constitutional Convention

• Nationwide election of 202 historic delegates is conducted across the islands.
• Tasked with drafting the framework for the historic 1935 Constitution.
• Laid down the legal foundation for the upcoming Commonwealth Government.

July 11, 1869

Birth of Dr. Pío Valenzuela

• Born in Polo, Bulacan—the municipality later renamed Valenzuela City in his honor.
• Served as an essential physician and inner secret chamber leader of the Katipunan.
• Managed the printing and logistics of the revolutionary paper, *Ang Kalayaan*.

July 11, 1860

Birth of Gen. Pío del Pilar

• Born in Culi-culi, San Pedro de Macati (modern-day Pio del Pilar, Makati City).
• Rose to prominence as one of Emilio Aguinaldo's most trusted frontline generals.
• Actively distributed copies of Jose Rizal's *Noli Me Tángere* prior to the uprising.
